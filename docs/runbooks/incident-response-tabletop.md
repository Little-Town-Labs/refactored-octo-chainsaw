# Incident Response Tabletop

F24 tabletop evidence covers three Stage 8 readiness scenarios. Each exercise records facilitator, incident reference, timeline entries, evidence references, gaps, owners, due dates, and follow-up status.

## Scenario 1: cross-side leakage

1. Inject a synthetic privacy-filter/cross-side leakage signal.
2. Confirm severity is sev-1 and on-call is paged.
3. Open an incident and assign an incident commander.
4. Preserve audit event and match/dossier references.
5. Record awareness time and breach-notification review.
6. Document containment, recovery, postmortem, and corrective actions.

## Scenario 2: credential compromise

1. Start from suspected human or service credential compromise.
2. Revoke sessions or affected credentials using the credential lifecycle procedure.
3. Review credential and operator audit events.
4. Open an incident if seeker or employer data may have been accessed.
5. Preserve evidence references and complete personal-data access review.

## Scenario 3: monitoring/deadline failure

1. Create a personal-data incident with GDPR applicability.
2. Advance the clock near and beyond the 72-hour deadline.
3. Confirm approaching and overdue deadline signals.
4. Record counsel escalation, blocked/missed cause, recovery, and corrective action.

## Evidence Checklist

- Incident key and commander
- Monitoring signal IDs
- Evidence references and hashes
- Notification obligations and deadlines
- Timeline entries
- Gaps and follow-up owners
